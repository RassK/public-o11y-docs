After completing :ref:`phase1`, you are ready for phase 2, pilot rollout. In the pilot rollout phase, your focus is to onboard your internal teams to Splunk Observability Cloud. These teams represent use cases that can be used to show the power and benefit of Splunk Observability Cloud to the rest of the organization.
